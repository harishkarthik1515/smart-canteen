# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

Our community is dedicated to providing an open and welcoming environment. All contributors are expected to follow this code of conduct.

Standards
- Be respectful and civil to other community members.
- Be considerate in language and tone.
- Refrain from demeaning or harassing behavior and speech.

Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the repository maintainers.

Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers, who will take appropriate action.
